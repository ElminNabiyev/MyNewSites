Polat Alemdar

Polat Alemdar is the biological son of Mehmet Karahanlı and Nergiz Karahanlı. Mehmet Karahanlı was an intelligence officer who provided many services to the state. He had carried out many successful operations, especially with Aslan Akbey. However, Karahanli, who was blinded by money and power in his last duties; He betrayed Israel by reporting that weapons were hidden in a refugee camp for Palestinians. Thereupon, the Israeli camp was bombed. Aslan Akbey's brother and his brother's fiancee, who were in the camp at the time, died. To take revenge, Aslan Bey, also known as Uncle, kidnapped Karahanlı's son, Efe Yakup Karahanlı, and gave him to the Child Protection Agency. When he sneezes like his father Mehmet Karahanlı, he sneezes 3 times in a row. Only Aslan Akbey knows this secret. A couple named Ömer Candan and Nazife Candan wanted to adopt a child from the Child Protection Agency. The uncle arranged for Efe Yakup to be given up for adoption to this couple. Uncle's purpose; It was to prevent Efe Yakup from being found by his real mother and father again by hiding in an unknown family. Ömer and Nazife Candan couple named Efe Yakup Ali. Thus, Mehmet Karahanlı's son, Efe Yakup Karahanlı, became Ali Candan. When Ali Candan grew up, he graduated from Ankara University Faculty of Political Sciences and became a diplomat. Although Ali Candan looked like a diplomat, he was an intelligence officer and served Aslan Akbey. Ali Candan has performed successful operations in many European countries. He used the code name Hikmet in many foreign operations.

Süleyman Çakır

He is of Laz origin.When he was 7 years old, he saw his father shot in front of him in the mosque courtyard, and because of this incident, he had a phobia of mosques. After his father died, he tried to support both his sister Derya and his mother Emine Çakır by selling the rice and chickpeas his mother made at home. One day, the police came and beat both him and his sister for selling in a forbidden place. He became furious after the police officer hit his sister during the harassment, and he stabbed the police officer repeatedly and killed him. He was later convicted of murder and imprisoned. When he was imprisoned, his mother and sister were left out in the open. For this reason, he was asked to stab Duran Şatıroğlu, aka Duran Emmi. He agreed to this because his mother and sister were in a bad situation. Duran Emmi went to kill him with a knife in his hand while he was ablution, but he couldn't do it when he saw his father dying while he was performing ablution. Duran Emmi became his mentor after this incident and took care of his family. Thanks to him, Derya was able to study architecture. He joined the mafia through Duran Emmi and started smuggling cigarettes. Then he married Laz Ziya's daughter Nesrin Yılmaz and made a name for himself in Kabadayı.

İskender Büyük

He was born in 1955. He has diabetes. It is seen that he uses insulin injections from time to time in the series. He served at the Gendarmerie General Command with the rank of brigadier general. It takes its name from a metaphorical combination of Alexander the Great in history. In addition, it is seen that the character in the series is similar to the historical Alexander the Great in many points in terms of type and characteristics. He is a government official who has undertaken numerous actions on behalf of the state during operations in Northern Iraq. He made an unsuccessful assassination attempt against Abdullah Öcalan in the Bekaa Valley. After his location was revealed in the movie, he planned and carried out the assassination of Turgut Özal with the guidance of Bülent Fuat Aras, Gladio's second man in Turkey. He would later be expelled from the army on the grounds that he was involved in illegal activities. He continues his illegal activities after retirement, but he never loses contact with both the army and other security units within the state. He also organized an unsuccessful assassination attempt against the Prime Minister of Turkey. He has a daughter named Funda İlmen from his relationship with Sevtap Özdoğan İlmen. Later, he kills Funda's mother by smothering her with a pillow in the hospital. Throughout the series, he kills many people who oppose him. He entered into a big fight with Polat Alemdar after he killed the Head of Intelligence. He tried to capture the Red Book before Polat Alemdar, but failed. In response, he kidnapped Polat Alemdar's closest man, Memati Baş, and tortured him by giving him heroin. He shot down General Sadi Pasha's helicopter with a rocket launcher, and had Brigadier General Mesut Akdağ killed in his office by using a hitman. He burned down Deli Hikmet's house, killed Polat's men Nevzat Yakışırbey, Hasan Pürmüz and Eren Eylül, and cut off one of Güllü Erhan's arms. He took Polat Alemdar captive and tried to make him tell what was written in the Book.Accordingly, it was used by Gladio in different periods.

Ersoy Ulubey

He is a civil servant and intelligence officer who served for years in the east and southeast under the Gendarmerie Intelligence.He joined the Gendarmerie Intelligence and Counter-Terrorism unit, which was established by a gendarmerie colonel in the southeast, with the will of the state but unofficially, to fight the separatist terrorist organization by waging a guerrilla war, among names such as İskender Büyük. They thought that a radical Islamist group that emerged among the Sunni segment in the southeast could be useful. They contributed to the establishment of the Hizbullah (Domuzbağcılar)organization by helping them arm themselves and grow. They enabled Hezbollah (Domuzbağcılar) to engage in significant conflicts with the separatist terrorist organization. He served in the fight against Asala unit for a while. Ersoy Ulubey is the head of the intelligence wing of the former southeastern deep state organization. He is the current manager of the organization, which has become ineffective after its active work has ended and most of the executive staff has been exposed.His son was murdered by terrorists during the raid on his house. Thereupon he erased the state from his mind. He began to act autonomously by binding the confessors and guards under state protection to himself. İskender Büyük felt danger due to Ersoy Ulubey's unstable behavior and sent him to the mountains where various organizations were located, under the name of duty, to be killed. Ersoy Ulubey managed to survive every time. He got involved in drug and smuggled cigarette business with his intelligence officers. He formed an army of 1000 people, such as confessors and guards, brought from the Southeast and always under his command, and camped them in Istanbul. He appointed Hamit, a former organizer but later a confessor and working for intelligence, as the head of this army. Polat Alemdar raided this camp and destroyed part of the army. However, Ersoy Ulubey also caused great harm to Polat. He killed Memati's son and shot Polat's mother, then he was caught, beaten to death with an ax by Polat and Memati, and thrown down from the Police Department building.

He is a very intelligent and aristocratic character.In his youth, he was an intelligence officer trained by Doğu Eşrefoğlu. He served in various positions on behalf of the Turkish Resistance Organization in Cyprus with his partner Aslan Akbey. The most important of these duties is to arm the Turkish Resistance Organization. He distributed weapons on the island in his role as an orange trader.Mehmet Karahanlı's only weakness is his son Efe Yakup Karahanlı, who was kidnapped when he was a child. His only failure in life is that he cannot find his son. He changed sides and betrayed the state due to his weakness for power during his second mission to destroy the secret forces, which was his second mission taken by Doğu Bey after the Cyprus Operation. He is a great patriot.He graduated from the Military Academy.[3] He is an intelligence officer trained by Doğu Eşrefoğlu in his youth. Together with his partner Mehmet Karahanlı, he served in many positions, especially in the Turkish Resistance Organization. Israel bombed these camps as a result of Karakhanid denouncing the Turkish intelligence officers in the camp in Palestine in order to declare his loyalty to the Masonic organization, which he betrayed and joined during his last mission.Among these camps are Aslan Akbey's brother and his brother's pregnant girlfriend. Unable to handle both his partner's betrayal and the murder of his brother and his brother's lover, he kidnapped Mehmet Karahanlı's son, Efe Yakup Karahanlı, and after years of training, he trained him as a patriot and made him an intelligence officer affiliated with the KGT unit. This operation was called Operation Valley of the Wolves. Through Polat, he managed to sow discord within the Council and collapse it from within. By ensuring the failure of two schemes established by the council members on different dates for the purpose of arms and drug trade, he caused the disappearance of the figures close to the Karakhanid, first Tombalacı Mehmet and then Sawere Necmi.

Zaza Dayı

As his name suggests, he is a character of Zaza origin, so he is also known as Uncle Zaza. He is from Elazığ. He has a habit of constantly playing with the earlobes of the people around him, especially his closest man, Cevher. When he is dissatisfied or very happy, he asks the other person, "Don't you love me/Do you love me?" he says. His men often respond to him by saying, "I love you, uncle/Let those who do not love you die." While he was working for İskender Büyük, he was imprisoned for drug smuggling. However, after 25 years, he was released from prison with the help of İskender. He is influential in the streets because of the many nephews he has under his command. He is aware of everything that happens on the streets of Istanbul. His work is based more on theft than the mafia style, usually on purse-snatching and sometimes on smuggling. His rise and his name becoming a big name is thanks to İskender Büyük. Even though he did not like İskender Büyük very much after he was released from prison, he stood by him out of duty of loyalty. In the later episodes of the series, as the Iskender formation gradually collapsed, he ordered Zaza to hand over his money and the children under his command to the organization. Zaza was forced to fulfill this request, but then he fulfilled his duty of loyalty and did not go to help Polat Alemdar, even though he was aware of it when he came to catch him. Since he did not go despite Alexander the Great's call and risked his life, he raided Zaza's place with a large number of Aron Feller's men wearing ski masks and killed all of his men. When Polat Alemdar and intelligence officers came from behind, he escaped by helicopter before he could kill Zaza. Having lost their men and nephews, the relations between Zaza and Polat Alemdar, which were previously damaged due to İskender Büyük, have entered a process of rapprochement. Memati also left his tense relations with Zaza and became closer over time, even acting as a referee in the issue between Zülfikar Ağa and Zaza.
